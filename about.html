About Us

Renata Pacheco is a young Brazilian artist. She was born and raised in the state of Alagoas, located on the north-eastern coast of the country. When in University, she was always stood out for her talent with handmade architectural drawings and also for her particular way of seeing the world. Back in 2017, Renata moved to São Paulo for a short period to expose some of her oil paintings in a recently opened Art Gallery Exhibition. Renata was able to expose her art and grow her network, returning to Maceió with several personalized orders.



Through each stroke, Renata's paintings reveal her unique vision of the world, full of feelings and expression. The artist is also involved in social projects and she uses her art to bring attention to important subjects, such as a special painting she made in 2017 for Dr. Hemerson Casado, a cardio surgeon with ALS. With the support of a friend, Renata's painting gained life in a video, reporting what many patients with ALS experience.

Link to access the video through Instagram, you can find it here. Audio in English. Subtitles options: Portuguese, Spanish, Turkish and Russian.



Besides her artwork, Renata also works as volunteer in a non-profitable organization who rescue homeless animals (specially cats and dogs). Her passion for animals involuntary made her start bringing them to the canvas. In a special request, Renata was asked to create a portrait for a lovely girl. Another fountain of inspiration for Renata, are most certainly the sunsets. Living in Maceió, the magical Brazillian Caribbean beaches and Sea are always a source from which Renata can find inspiration.
